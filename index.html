Our Story

Three Generations of Trust

Vishram Spices (India) Pvt. Ltd. has been Mumbai's trusted wholesale partner since 1972. What began as a spice trading business in Darukhana has grown over three generations into a complete kitchen supplier for hotels, restaurants, and catering establishments across Maharashtra.

With 300+ products across 16 categories, spanning spices, masalas, dry fruits, oils, grains, sauces, and housekeeping essentials, we are the single source for everything a professional kitchen needs. Our Vishram brand products are packed to order, ensuring freshness on every delivery.
