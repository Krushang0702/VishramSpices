Our Story

Three Generations of Trust

Vishram Spices (India) Pvt. Ltd. has been Mumbai's trusted wholesale partner since 1972. What began as a humble spice trading business has grown over three generations into a comprehensive kitchen solutions supplier, serving hotels, restaurants, and catering establishments across Maharashtra.

With over 500 SKUs spanning spices, masalas, dry fruits, oils, grains, sauces, and housekeeping essentials, we are the single source for everything a professional kitchen needs. Our Vishram brand products are packed to order, ensuring freshness and quality on every delivery.

Today, Vishram Spices delivers across Maharashtra, serving hotels, restaurants, and catering establishments who trust us with their kitchen every single day.
